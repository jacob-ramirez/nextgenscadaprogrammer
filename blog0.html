Blog 0 – About Me

My name is Jacob Ramirez and I’m a computer programmer specializing in Ignition SCADA from Inductive Automation. I have been working in the oil field service industry for over 8 years now and working with Ignition for over 3 years. I went to college for Computer Science and completed my degree and never could find a software development job in the Permian Basin. So I then changed my focus over to automation and started out as an electrical apprentice then moved to automation technician (little bit PLC programming), then into my current role of SCADA developer. 

In this blog I will be covering a lot of How-To’s in using Ignition, as well as tips and tricks I have found along the way and my journey throughout my career. Also, showcase how learning computer programming has helped me a lot in developing in Ignition. I will share my research in the comparisons between other SCADA systems and Ignition and how Ignition is the best production for 2020+ in my opinion. A lot of other SCADA systems have not leveraged all the new technologies in today’s generation, hence why I use the name Next Gen SCADA Programmer for myself. I will also share the path I’m taking to learn DevOps and apply it to the SCADA world. I wanted to help share tips and short tutorials in the Ignition platform that I have found as well as maybe help others in choosing the best SCADA platform option for their use case. 

I am planning on updating the blog regularly, I’m shooting for once a month right now. I hope you find the content interesting and helpful. If not, shoot a comment below and suggest a topic that you would like me to cover and I’ll do my research on that, any How-To’s you’d like me to go in depth with or any pointers for creating an application in Ignition. Until next time, continue coding.
